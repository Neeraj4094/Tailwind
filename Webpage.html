    About Us

    Online MBA in Panchkula

    Online & Distance Learning Programs

    Since our establishment on November 27, 2002, as the ELL AAR MEMORIAL EDUCATIONAL Trust (Registration no. 1563), we’ve been dedicated to shaping minds and empowering individuals.

    Our ISO 9001:2015 certified institute offers a wide range of online & distance learning programs, from Para-Medical, IT, and various other professional courses like Online MBA in Panchkula. Our mission is simple yet powerful, to provide you with the highest education and training standards.

    As a registered Vocational Training Provider (VTP) under the Skill Development Initiative Scheme by the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, Government of India, we take great pride in participating in the journey towards skill development.

    We have successfully launched numerous vocational training programs with our Training Provider (TP) ID 004326 and Training Centre (TC) ID 041525 for PMKVY 2.0.

    We not only focus on imparting valuable skills but also on creating opportunities for successful trainees. Our efforts extend beyond training, as we assist our beneficiaries in shaping their careers by connecting them with relevant job placements in their chosen fields. Additionally, we offer specialized training in areas such as Medical, Nursing, Electronics, and more.

    Here at ELL AAR Institute of Management & Technology, we have proudly educated and empowered thousands of students, enabling them to reach new heights in their professions. Our commitment lies in delivering quality education that not only helps students grasp concepts with clarity but also equips them with practical skills.

    At ELL AAR Institute, education and training unite to open doors to new opportunities. Explore the possibilities and unlock your potential with us.
